About us

"Well Done is Better Than Well Said"
- Benjamin Franklin

Chris Chros Films has proudly been the foundation of the ad film production industry since 2004. Over the past 20 years, we've had the privilege of producing more than 500 advertisement films in association with some of India's leading ad agencies.

We have worked with the top TVC directors in India and internationally renowned filmmakers such as Shashank Khaitan, Farah Khan, Anurag Kashyap, Tarun Mansukhani, Prabhudeva, and Reema Kagti. Our wide ranging and extensive experience includes international shoots in diverse and exotic locations like Tunisia, Georgia, the Philippines, South Africa, Malaysia, Romania, Thailand, Dubai, and Sri Lanka.

We have earned many prestigious and valuable awards, including Spike Asia Awards, a Kyoorius Award, and Abby Awards.

By collaborating with brilliant creative minds and outstanding professional talents across various departments, we have helped people creating many advertisements and telling people stories.

Each film we produce fills us with pride and provides a new and improved experience, creating lasting impact and, most importantly, earning the trust of our clients, who continue to return to us. We are deeply grateful to everyone who has contributed to our journey, from our spot boys to our directors, agency creatives, and clients.
