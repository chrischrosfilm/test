About us

"Well Done is Better Than Well Said"
- Benjamin Franklin

Chris Chros Films has been an integral part of the ad film industry since 2004. Over the past 20 years, we've had the privilege of producing more than 500 advertisement films in association with some of India's leading ad agencies.

We have worked renowned filmmakers such as Shashank Khaitan, Farah Khan, Anurag Kashyap, Tarun Mansukhani, Prabhudeva, Reema Kagti and international known directors of the likes of Ben Hartenstien, Shwetabh Varma, Narayan Shi (NARU), Rahul Srivastav, Anand iyer (ANDY), Shashank Khaitan, Rajneesh Ghai (RAZY). Our wide ranging and extensive experience includes international shoots in diverse and exotic locations like Tunisia, Georgia, the Philippines, South Africa, Malaysia, Romania, Thailand, Dubai, and Sri Lanka.

We have earned many prestigious and valuable awards, including Spike Asia Awards, a Kyoorius Award, and Abby Awards.

Each film we produce fills us with pride and provides us with a new and improved experience, creating lasting impact and, most importantly, earning the trust and loyalty of our clients. We are deeply grateful to everyone who has contributed to our journey, from our spot boys to our directors, agency creatives, and clients.
